Specialized Charts

Treemap, Sunburst, Heatmap, Waterfall, Range, Funnel, and Pyramid visualizations

1. Treemap

Hierarchical data as nested rectangles. Shows company headcount by department and team.

2. Sunburst

Hierarchical data as concentric rings. Shows revenue breakdown by region, country, and product.

3. Heatmap

Matrix visualization with colour intensity. Shows monthly temperature data across years.

4. Waterfall

Cumulative effect of sequential values. Shows financial breakdown from revenue to net income.

5. Range Bar

Bars with low and high values. Shows project task durations across a timeline.

6. Range Area

Shaded area between min and max values. Shows monthly temperature ranges throughout the year.

7. Funnel

Progressive reduction through stages. Shows a sales pipeline from leads to closed deals.

8. Pyramid

Triangular representation of hierarchical levels. Shows organizational structure by employee count.
